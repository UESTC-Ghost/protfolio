Project Background

In many agricultural regions, wild bear attacks pose a significant threat to both production safety and human lives. Traditional monitoring methods lack real-time alerts and efficient deterrence mechanisms.

We developed "BaiZe" (LUTBY), a comprehensive system combining Deep Learning, Embedded Systems, and Cross-Platform Applications. The system achieves all-weather monitoring, real-time identification, and automated expulsion of bears, leveraging edge computing on the Nvidia Jetson Nano platform.

Key Technologies
Advanced AI Model

Improved YOLOv8 architecture by integrating DCNv3 (Deformable Convolution) and Transformer self-attention modules to enhance detection accuracy in complex field environments.

Embedded System

Deployed on Nvidia Jetson Nano. Integrated HTD35H servos for omnidirectional monitoring, USB cameras for capture, and sirens/lights for active deterrence.

LoRa Communication

Implemented LoRa technology for long-range, low-power communication (1-5km coverage), ensuring reliable alerts even in remote areas without cellular coverage.
